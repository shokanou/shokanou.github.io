Project Description

Kiely provides an online platform to help connect Aalto students who want to practice a new language and make friends at the same time in a delightful experience.

It offers:

1. Easy to use online browse experience to help Aalto students find other students to practice and learn languages with

2. A simple algorithm to help students find language partners that are most likely to also have other similar interests

3. A built-in chat interface for hassle-free communication


User Group and Use Cases


User Groups

Group 1: Primarily interested in learning a language. They care about easily browsing people with the appropriate language.

Group 2:Interested in networking and socializing, with language being a secondary motivation. They care about looking at people’s profiles for more information.
